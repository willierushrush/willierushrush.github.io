I'm Will, a fifth year PhD student in mathematics at Stony Brook University, NY. I specialise in dynamical systems, in particular complex dynamics and renormalization theory. My advisor is Dzmitry Dudko.

I was born and raised in the tropics of Indonesia, more precisely Jakarta, the big durian and unfortunately the fastest sinking city in the world. The teenage me grew up in the little red dot of Singapore and then I moved out to London, UK, where I spent my college/uni years. I earned my MSci degree in pure mathematics from Imperial College London in 2019.
